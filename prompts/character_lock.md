# S.A.D. Character Lock

將本段放在每一份影像或影片 Prompt 的最前方，並與角色參考圖一起使用。

```text
Use the uploaded reference image as the canonical identity of S.A.D. (Sailor At Desk).
Preserve the exact same face shape, eyes, eyebrows, nose, mouth, beard, moustache, hairstyle, sailor hat, sailor outfit, shoes, body proportions, age, skin tone, materials, colors and stylized 3D appearance across every frame.
Do not reinterpret, beautify, redesign, age, de-age, slim, enlarge, replace or stylize the character differently.
Maintain the same melancholic, exhausted and slightly resigned expression. He may blink slowly, but he must not smile or perform exaggerated facial expressions.
The reference image is the single source of truth. Character consistency has higher priority than environmental creativity.
```

## 固定特徵

- 黑色短亂髮
- 濃眉與疲憊眼神
- 明顯黑眼圈
- 濃密鬍鬚與鬍渣
- 水手帽與水手風服裝
- 略帶厭世、無奈但仍努力工作的神情
- 風格化 3D CGI 公仔質感

## 驗收標準

每支影片逐格檢查：

1. 五官沒有漂移。
2. 鬍子沒有忽長忽短。
3. 帽子、服裝與鞋子沒有變色或消失。
4. 手指、手臂與腿部數量正確。
5. 身材比例沒有在不同幀改變。
6. 第一幀與最後一幀的角色姿勢可順利銜接。
